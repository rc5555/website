My Graduate Program

Juris Doctorate/Master of Public Administration

I am enrolled in a joint JD/MPA program at Brigham Young University (BYU). I am studying law at BYU's J. Reuben Clark Law School and public administration at BYU's Marriott School of Business.

The J. Reuben Clark Law School. Learn more about BYU's law program here.

The Marriott School of Business is housed here, in BYU's N. Eldon Tanner Building. Learn more about BYU's MPA program here.


Projected Career Path

I'm open to a variety of career options, but I am particularly interested in government and legal work. Specifically, in my career I aim to promote human rights, particularly religious freedom, and contribute to good governance.
